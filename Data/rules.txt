**Mafia Game Rules:**
1. **Objective:** The Town must eliminate all Mafia members and the Serial Killer. The Mafia must outnumber the Town. The Serial Killer must be the last player standing.
2. **Phases:** The game alternates between Day and Night phases.
3. **Day Phase:** Players discuss and vote to lynch someone. Voting is done via the `/vote` command.
4. **Night Phase:** Mafia members choose a player to kill. The Serial Killer also chooses a player to kill. Some Town roles may have night actions.
5. **Voting:** During the Day phase, players can vote for another player using the `/vote @player` command. The player with the most votes at the end of the Day phase is lynched.
6. **Role Actions:** Some roles have special actions that can be performed during the Night phase. These actions are communicated to the bot via DM.
7. **Winning:** The game ends when one faction achieves its win condition.
8. **No Meta-Gaming:** Do not discuss the game outside of the designated channels or after you are dead.
9. **No Sharing Role Information:** Do not share screenshots or verbatim copies of messages from the bot regarding your role or actions.
10. **Inactivity:** Players who are inactive for extended periods may be removed from the game.
11. **Have Fun:** Remember, it's just a game! Enjoy the experience and the company of your fellow players.
